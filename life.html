Hot Plate



There are several flavors that life has,

The same meal has several palates,

But it is the armed armies,

That can destroy an entire nation.


They mock at the innocence of those who came,

They infuse the already shaped mental chain,

They make the new specie mute,

That the same stanzas sang addicted.
